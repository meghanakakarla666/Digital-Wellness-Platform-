ðŸ’¡ Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I exercise for optimal health? +
The CDC recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week, plus muscle-strengthening activities on 2 or more days per week.
What's the best diet for weight loss? +
The best diet is one you can stick to long-term. Focus on creating a moderate calorie deficit through a balanced diet rich in whole foods, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Consult with a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
How many hours of sleep do I need? +
Most adults need 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night. Individual needs may vary, but consistently getting less than 7 hours can impact your health, mood, and cognitive function.
How can I manage stress effectively? +
Effective stress management includes regular exercise, adequate sleep, healthy eating, mindfulness practices, social connections, and time management. Consider professional help if stress becomes overwhelming.
What are the most important health screenings? +
Key screenings include blood pressure, cholesterol, blood sugar, BMI, cancer screenings (mammogram, colonoscopy, skin checks), and regular check-ups with your healthcare provider. Timing depends on age and risk factors.

Stay Hydrated
Drink water throughout the day. Aim for 8 glasses, more if you're active or in hot weather.
